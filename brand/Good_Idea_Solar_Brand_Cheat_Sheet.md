# Good Idea Solar — Brand Cheat Sheet

*The one-page north star. Keep this open while designing. (Full detail lives in the Brand Guide.)*

---

### Positioning
**Core message:** Solar for the Farm, Not the Farm for Solar
**Founding idea:** Solar should be another crop to support the farm — let the farmer "harvest the sun."
**Who we are:** A farmer-first solar development company. We help farms create long-term income through solar projects while keeping farmland productive and supporting local communities.

### Hero
> **Solar for the Farm, Not the Farm for Solar**
> Good Idea Solar partners with family farms to create long-term income through solar development while preserving productive farmland and strengthening local communities.
> [View Our Projects] · [Contact Us]

### Mission
Help farms remain productive, economically viable, and connected to their communities through responsible solar development.

### Core Values
- **Service** — We exist to help farmers and communities succeed.
- **Trust** — We build long-term relationships through transparency and follow-through.
- **Partnership** — We work alongside landowners, communities, and customers to create lasting value.

### Message Hierarchy (always in this order)
1. Productive Farmland 2. Long-Term Income 3. Community Benefit 4. Renewable Energy
*Solar is the solution. The farm is the priority.*

### The Story in One Line
Founder Justin Aydelotte inherited a family farm that was losing money; putting a small portion into solar kept it profitable and in the family — and became a mission to help other farmers do the same.

### Proof Points (the case we make)
- The average small family farm **loses ~$4/acre/year** from farming; small family farms own **~89% of U.S. farmland.**
- Our method converts a **portion** of the farm and can earn a farm **up to $20,000/acre/year at no cost**, with **below-market** energy for the community.
- *(Use these sparingly on the public site; lead with the farmer outcome, not the dollar figure. Keep detailed financials off the site.)*

### Voice
**We are:** ✓ Practical ✓ Approachable ✓ Honest ✓ Competent ✓ Service-Oriented ✓ Farmer-Focused
**We are not:** ✗ Corporate ✗ Technical for technical's sake ✗ Pushy ✗ Political ✗ Sales-driven ✗ Buzzword-heavy

**Write like this:** "We partner with farmers." · "We help farms create long-term income." · "We'd be happy to discuss your property."
**Not like this:** "Leveraging innovative renewable energy solutions." · "Unlocking stakeholder value." · "Accelerating the energy transition."

### Color Palette (confirmed against the master logo art)
| Blue | Green | Yellow | Black | Charcoal | Light Gray | White |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| `#143B90` | `#007562` | `#FFF200` | `#000000` | `#1D1F1F` | `#E7E6E6` | `#FFFFFF` |

Ratio: ~60% White · 25% Blue · 10% Green · 5% Yellow.
**Buttons:** Blue `#143B90` fill, white text. **Never** use yellow for text on white (fails contrast) — yellow is fill/accent only. Logo & wordmark are pure black `#000000`; body text is Charcoal `#1D1F1F`.

### Logo
- **Primary (horizontal):** header, footer, documents, presentations → `final-01.jpg` / `Branding/final-01.png` (vector: `final.ai/.eps/.pdf`).
- **Secondary (meatball):** social media, favicon, mobile nav, profile images → `Branding/Meatball.jpg`.
- Sun + green hill + blue panel-grid field in a black circle. Use on white/light gray. Don't stretch, rotate, recolor, add shadows, or place on busy photos.

### Imagery
**Show:** ✓ Farms ✓ People ✓ Projects ✓ Communities ✓ Real team members ✓ Real project sites

Mood: Authentic · Rural · Competent. **Use the real photos in `/Images` first** (sheep, crops, peppers, soy).
